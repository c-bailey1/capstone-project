Copilot Practice Activity

Goal:
Get comfortable using Copilot (standalone) to summarize information.

What to Do:

  • Pick any topic you’re interested in.
  • Find an article or webpage about it.
  • Ask Copilot standalone to summarize the information.
  • Change your prompt once to see how the summary changes.

No submission required.
This activity is just for practice and exploration.
